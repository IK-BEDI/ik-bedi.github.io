Introduction
Brief Profile

I am a dedicated professional with a PhD in Educational Science, specialising in Educational Leadership & Management, which I earned from the prestigious University of Tartu, Estonia. My passion lies in teaching, research methods, psychology, and teacher education.

I am strongly inclined to uncover knowledge through quantitative research design, although I also possess valuable experience in qualitative research approaches. My primary interests revolve around data analytics and leadership and management research, particularly in education and industry.

As an avid data enthusiast, I have leveraged statistical tools such as SPSS, R, Excel, SQL, Power BI, and Pivot tables to derive meaningful insights from data. My research delves into factors that can enhance school leadership practices, investigating the sources of stress among school leaders, and exploring mental health issues affecting educational leaders.

I bring a wealth of experience in conducting research and have a track record of publications in reputable journals, including those indexed in Scopus and other databases. I am open to venturing into the business world, leveraging my strong work ethic, analytics, creativity, diligence, and unwavering dedication to contribute to the holistic growth of any business organization or educational institution.

Collaboration and teamwork are integral to my approach, as I firmly believe that collective efforts lead to success and innovation.
